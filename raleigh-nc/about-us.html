About Us

SafeAir Certified Mold Inspection Inc.

SafeAir Certified Mold Inspection specializes in mold testing in Atlanta, air quality testing, consultation, and analysis of residential and commercial properties. The mold testing and mold inspection services we provide are used by individuals who know or believe they may have a mold problem. All SafeAir Certified mold Inspectors are Council-certified Microbial Consultants through the ACAC, the industry leader for certification for the mold testing and inspection industry.
